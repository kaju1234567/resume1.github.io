Islamic
Religious Festivals

There are several Islamic festivals that provide time for Muslims to reflect and celebrate their faith.
The two most commonly celebrated festivals are Eid-ul_Fitr and Eid-ul-adha.

Eid-ul-Fitr

Eid al-Fitr - which means 'festival of the breaking of the fast - is celebrated at the end of Ramadan, , a month when many adult Muslims fast.

Eid-ul-Adha

Eid al-Fitr - which means 'festival of the breaking of the fast - is celebrated at the end of Ramadan, , a month when many adult Muslims fast.
