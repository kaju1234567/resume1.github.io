This English language Professional Certificate Programme is aimed at students with an intermediate level of English (based on the B1 level of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) wishing to advance their language skills to an upper-intermediate level (B2).

This Programme will cover interesting topics such as food, business, modern life, globalization and technology today. You will learn to write different types of formal and informal texts, including reports, reviews, complaints and more. You will also enhance your vocabulary and grammar and better understand how to use verbal tenses in context.

This Programme also includes preparatory materials and recommendations to successfully pass the Cambridge University First Certificate in English Examination (FCE).
